PeerShape Pro: Community skills for community problems

We believe that every good idea deserves to become a reality. PeerShape Pro or "PeerShape Projects" is the next step in the evolution of crowd sourcing, bringing together community resources to shape ideas and solve community problems.

Ideas that come from the bottom-up capture the diverse perspectives, challenges and opportunities of the entire community. And when good ideas does not become a reality, we as a society lose out. We saw that non-profits, neighbors, volunteers, and companies needed a better way...a better way to give shape and structure to an idea, to find the necessary resources, to collaborate with each other, and to execute the idea into a reality. This is why we started PeerShape.

So, how does PeerShape Pro work? It has the following simple steps.. .

Post Idea: Originators describe their idea and start a list of goals, tasks, and needs

Shape Project: The originator's community (whether it is coworkers, volunteers, or neighbors) collaborates to shape the idea and organize it into an executable project.

Match Resources: Contributions (time, materials & money) are matched to goals and tasks.

Manage Project: Simple, yet powerful tool that organizes the work of many to deliver the project. So, where all can PeerShape Pro be used? Here are some examples

Community Projects: From creating a community garden to repairing a broken playground or building a ramp for a disabled neighbor, bring together your community to solve a real problem.

Organize Events: Host neighborhood gatherings, music festivals or scientific conferences - open doors and embrace the culture, heritage and arts that shape your community.

Build & Create: Bring minds together and create something wonderful. Create a new product or the next big start-up, leveraging the strengths of your community.

Connect Organizations: Whether it is across company offices or an industry cluster; across non-profit, corporate, or government sectors, ensure that the best ideas find the resources to become reality.
